# Roadmap

The roadmap now lives on the GitHub issue tracker, grouped by version milestone:

- **v0.2.x** — first real corpus and first fine-tune. [Open issues](https://github.com/itsmeduncan/civic-slm/milestone/1).
- **v0.3.x** — generalization across U.S. jurisdictions. [Open issues](https://github.com/itsmeduncan/civic-slm/milestone/2).
- **v1.0** — long-term-supported release. [Open issues](https://github.com/itsmeduncan/civic-slm/milestone/3).

Roadmap items are labeled [`roadmap`](https://github.com/itsmeduncan/civic-slm/issues?q=label%3Aroadmap+is%3Aopen). Anything not labeled `roadmap` is regular issue traffic (bugs, smaller enhancements, questions).

To influence priority, comment on the relevant issue or open a new one. See `CONTRIBUTING.md` for the dev workflow.

Shipped milestones live in `CHANGELOG.md`.
